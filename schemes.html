🏛 Government Schemes For Farmers

🌾 PM-KISAN

Financial assistance of ₹6000 per year to eligible farmers.

Benefit: Direct income support.

💳 Kisan Credit Card

Provides short-term credit for agricultural needs.

Benefit: Easy farm loans.

🛡 PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme for farmers.

Benefit: Protection against crop loss.

🌱 Soil Health Card

Provides soil testing reports and recommendations.

Benefit: Better crop productivity.

🚜 Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

Financial support for agriculture infrastructure projects.

Benefit: Modern farming facilities.

💧 PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Promotes irrigation and water management.

Benefit: Improved water efficiency.
